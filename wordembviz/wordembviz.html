Visualizing Word Embeddings

This page hosts a Jupyter Notebook for visualizing word embeddings using t-SNE in sklearn and adjustText.


Downloads:

 

  • wordembviz.zip: notebook for generating visualizations along with partial GloVe embedding files (24MB)
  • wordembviz-glove.pdf: pdf visualization of GloVe embeddings
  • wordembviz-glove-png.zip: zoomed-in parts of full visualization
  • wordembviz-animated.zip: animated GIFs and PPTX for generating animations (22MB)
